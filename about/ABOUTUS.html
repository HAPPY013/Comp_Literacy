ABOUT US

CompLit exist to impact people without computer skills by offering them a web-guide on how to use a computer. The website will assist those who are struggling with any part of the computer, both external and internal,: External simply includes assisting on how to use a mouse/trackpad, etc; then Internal includes assisting on how to create email and google docs. We are aware of the extreme shift to digital world, yet other people are not familiar with this digital world. This platform will help as many people out there who are still left behind.

Mission

Our mission is to provide people who lack computer skills with the knowlegde and skills they need in order to perform basic tasks in a computer.

Vision

Our vision is to upskill people with basic computer skills so that the number of computer illiterate people change.
